From your first employee's first day you have pension duties. If a worker qualifies, you must put them into a workplace pension and pay in at least the minimum employer contribution. You must also complete a declaration of compliance with The Pensions Regulator within the statutory window after your duties start — this is the deadline first-time employers most often miss. Your dashboard tracks the exact date.
