Almost every worker must be paid at least the minimum wage for their age band, and apprentices have their own rate. The rates change every April. The current figures for your account are always taken from the live statutory configuration — never from memory. If someone's pay is below their band's floor, that is illegal from the day the rate changes, and the fix is a recorded pay variation.
