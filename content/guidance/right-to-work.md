Before anyone starts you must check they can legally work in the UK and keep dated proof. A British or Irish passport is checked by hand; anyone with an eVisa or biometric permit is checked on the free GOV.UK service with a share code. A correct, recorded check gives you a statutory excuse — a complete legal defence. Check everyone the same way.
